A series of 3 courses that teach participants how to represent and manipulate data using linear algebra, perform principal component analysis, solve optimization problems, and train models for data description including simple neural networks.

Statistics with Python Specialization

Learn basic and intermediate concepts of statistical analysis using Python programming language to conduct data analyses, including data collection, summary and visualization, estimation, assessing theories, inferential results interpretation, and advanced statistical modeling.

Probability Theory for Data Science

This course teaches the foundations of probability, including discrete and continuous random variables, and their relationship to statistics and data science. It is part of CU Boulder's Master of Science in Data Science degree offered on Coursera.
